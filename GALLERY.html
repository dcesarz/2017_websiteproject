Mount Dawson, elevation 3,377 metres (11,079 ft), is a massif in the Selkirk Mountains of British Columbia, Canada, located in Glacier National Park to the southeast of Rogers Pass. It is a double summit, the highest of which is Hasler Peak, the second peak being Feuz Peak, with Hasler Peak's elevation making it the highest summit in the range. Mount Dawson is ranked as the 58th highest mountain in British Columbia.The Dawson Glacier is located on its eastern flank.(Source: Wikipedia)
